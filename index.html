A Vigil of Remembrance

In Memory of Josiah

Forever Beloved

HOSS  ·  Class of 1998

Order of Service

🕊

Section 1

Welcome & Opening

  • Host greeting
  • Why we are gathered
  • Housekeeping — mute mics, chat use
  • Moment of silence  (1–2 minutes)
📖

Section 2

Opening Reading & Prayer

Scripture Reading — Psalm 121:1–2

"I lift up my eyes to the hills —
where does my help come from?
My help comes from the Lord,
the Maker of heaven and earth."

"In our grief, we lift our eyes — not to escape the pain, but to remember that help comes from a source greater than our sorrow. Psalm 121 reminds us that even in moments like this, we are held, watched over, and not alone."

🤝

Section 3

Tribute & Sharing

  • A tribute from a close friend or family member
  • Open sharing from anyone who wishes to speak
  • No time limits — share freely from the heart
  • Chat remains open for written tributes, memories & blessings
🎵

Section 4

Music & Reflection

  • 1–2 songs or instrumental pieces
  • Cameras on if comfortable
  • Brief moment of silence
🕯

Section 5

Collective Ritual

  1. A minute of silence held together
  2. Everyone types a blessing or Josiah's name in the chat
  3. Group response together:
"We remember you."
✨

Section 6

Closing Words

  • Final words from the leader
  • Mention next steps — support fund, memorial page, etc.
  • Final moment of silence
  • Gentle goodbye
❦   ❦   ❦
"May his memory be a blessing
and his light continue through us."
Closing Tribute

Held in our hearts, always.

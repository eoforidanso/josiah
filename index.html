Order of Service

🕊

Josiah

Josiah was the quiet strength in every room — the one who made you feel seen without saying a word. He loved people deeply, laughed easily, and lived generously. His warmth left a mark on everyone who had the honour of knowing him.

Section 2

Opening Reading & Prayer

Scripture Reading — Psalm 121:1–2

"I lift up my eyes to the hills —
where does my help come from?
My help comes from the Lord,
the Maker of heaven and earth."

Read by  Fred Asante

"In our grief, we lift our eyes — not to escape the pain, but to remember that help comes from a source greater than our sorrow. Psalm 121 reminds us that even in moments like this, we are held, watched over, and not alone."

Scripture Reading — Romans 14:7–8

"For none of us lives for ourselves alone,
and none of us dies for ourselves alone.
If we live, we live for the Lord;
and if we die, we die for the Lord.
So, whether we live or die, we belong to the Lord."

Read by  Eric Kumah
